Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Who We Are

PA CareRate is a service of Accel Direct LLC ("we," "us," "our"), a business services company that connects caregivers with home care agencies through automated lead generation. We are located at 502 W 7th St, Ste 100, Erie, PA 16502.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our intake forms, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Employment-related information you provide (county, insurance type, authorized hours)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Evaluate your eligibility for caregiver placement opportunities
  • Present personalized offer information (pay rate, sign-on bonus)
  • Contact you by SMS and email regarding opportunities that match your profile
  • Connect you with partner home care agencies for potential employment

SMS Communications

By submitting your phone number through our intake form, you may opt in to receive text messages from PA CareRate / Accel Direct LLC related to caregiver placement opportunities. Two separate consent options are provided: one for non-marketing transactional messages (rate confirmations, specialist follow-ups, scheduling updates) and one for marketing and promotional messages (special offers, new opportunities, updates). Neither consent is required to use our services.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Upon opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

SMS Consent Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your SMS consent or phone number with any third parties for their marketing purposes. Your SMS opt-in consent is collected solely by PA CareRate (Accel Direct LLC) and is used exclusively to send you messages from PA CareRate. SMS consent information is not shared with partner agencies or any other third parties, except as required to deliver messages through our SMS service provider (LeadConnector/Twilio).

Information Sharing

We may share your non-SMS information (name, email, employment details) with partner home care agencies relevant to your placement opportunity, solely for the purpose of evaluating and onboarding you as a caregiver. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and intake forms may use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Ensure proper functioning of our website and forms
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our intake forms. We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information beyond what you voluntarily submit through our forms.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
